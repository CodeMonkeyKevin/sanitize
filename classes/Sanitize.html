REGEX_PROTOCOL = /^([^:]+)(?:\:|&#0*58|&#x0*3a)(?:[^0-9a-f]|$)/i   Matches an attribute value that could be treated by a browser as a URL with a protocol prefix, such as "http:" or "javascript:". Any string of one or more characters followed by a colon is considered a match, even if the colon is encoded as an entity and even if it‘s an incomplete entity (which IE6 and Opera will still parse).
